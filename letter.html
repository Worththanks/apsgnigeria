Letter of Invitation to the APC Progressive Support Group (APSG)
Inaugural Ceremony and Flag-Off for Asiwaju Le’kan Si 2027 Mobilization

Dear Hon. Cornelius Ojelabi,

On behalf of the leadership and members of the APC Progressive Support Group (APSG), we are honoured to invite you to our Inaugural Ceremony and Flag-Off for Asiwaju Le’kan Si 2027 Mobilization.

We humbly request your presence as a Distinguished Guest of Honour to inspire and elevate this landmark event, drawing on your exemplary leadership in guiding the APC in Lagos State.

Event Details:
- Theme: 2027 & Beyond – Preparation • Participation • Opportunities - Date: Thursday, February 12, 2026 - Time: 10:00 AM Prompt - Venue: Hoare’s Memorial Methodist Cathedral Church, Sabo, Yaba, Lagos State, Nigeria

APSG is an established political support and advocacy platform within the All Progressives Congress (APC), dedicated to promoting progressive ideals through grassroots mobilization, political education, youth empowerment, and leadership development.

This inauguration marks our formal launch as a national movement, focused on sustaining the progressive achievements under the leadership of President Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu GCFR as Nigeria looks toward 2027 and beyond to greater heights.
